Fulfilling Hunger, Nourishing Hope
FREE Food. Resources. for Everyone. Everywhere

At F.R.E.E, we believe that no one should have to go hungry. We are a non-profit organization based in Charlotte, dedicated to addressing the critical issues of hunger and food insecurity in our community.

What we do

Food bank

Every weekend, we open our doors to distribute non-perishable food items to those facing food insecurity. We believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right.

Grocery Partnerships

We collaborate with local grocers to issue food gift cards, providing individuals and families with the means to purchase essential groceries.

Support for the Homeless

We work closely with various organizations and sponsors to support the homeless population by delivering food directly to those in need on the streets.
